A glass pavilion above the Pacific.

Perched on a coveted stretch of coastline in La Jolla, this residence celebrates light, proportion, and uninterrupted ocean vistas. The main level is anchored by a voluminous great room, where retractable glass walls dissolve the boundary between the interior and an expansive terrace.

The chef’s kitchen features custom millwork, stone-clad counters, and integrated professional appliances, complemented by a concealed catering pantry for entertaining at scale. A floating staircase leads to thoughtfully separated bedroom wings, offering privacy for guests and family alike.

Outdoors, an infinity-edge pool blends visually with the horizon, accompanied by a fire feature, outdoor kitchen, and multiple lounge areas oriented to capture sunsets. Every finish—stone, glass, metal, and wood— has been curated in a restrained palette that amplifies the surrounding seascape.
